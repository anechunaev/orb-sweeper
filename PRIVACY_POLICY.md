# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-16_

## Overview

Orb Sweeper is a single-player game. It is designed with privacy in mind and operates entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

**Orb Sweeper does not collect any personal data.** Specifically:

- No personally identifiable information is gathered.
- No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking is performed.
- No device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data are accessed.
- No data is transmitted to us or to any third party.

## Advertising

Orb Sweeper does **not** display advertisements and does not integrate any advertising SDKs.

## Payments and Purchases

Orb Sweeper does **not** offer in-app purchases, subscriptions, or any form of payment.

## Third-Party Services

Orb Sweeper does **not** integrate with third-party services, social networks, or cloud providers.

## Local Storage

The game stores the following information locally on your device only. It never leaves your device:

- Game settings (audio, haptics, background, difficulty preferences).
- High scores and best times.

You can remove this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage through your device settings.

## Children's Privacy

Because Orb Sweeper collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published in this file along with a new "Last updated" date.
