## Full Description

Minesweeper has gone round. Orb Sweeper takes the puzzle you know and wraps it onto a little sphere made of hexagons. Every tap uncovers a new tile. Every number is a clue. Every mine is one wrong move away. Spin the sphere with your finger, zoom in to study a cluster, flag the danger, and clear the surface without a single misstep.

**Features**
- A fresh 3D spin on a timeless puzzle
- Smooth one-finger controls — spin, zoom, tap
- Three difficulty presets, plus fully custom games
- Guaranteed solvable puzzles with "No-Guess" mode
- Personal best times for every difficulty
- Your first tap is always safe
- Long-press to flag, tap a number to auto-clear its neighbors
- Clean, cozy visuals designed for phones
- Plays offline, free forever, no ads

Perfect for a coffee break, a commute, or that one quiet minute before bed.

How many orbs can you sweep?
